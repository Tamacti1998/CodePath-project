About the Event

The purpose of the ShuttleFlow website is to serve as a shuttle service booking platform for college and university students based on the school's existing shuttle system - essentially an Uber-like service, but specifically focused on providing transportation to and from campus, reducing waiting time, and improving efficiency.

The ride-finding form is a clean, intuitive interface built to help users quickly discover available rides in their area. At its core are clearly labeled input fields where users enter their pickup location, destination, and desired departure time. Once submitted, the form returns a list of matching rides and driver details. This form ensures a fast, hassle-free experience for users looking to connect with nearby drivers and get moving efficiently.
